About

Sara Warfield is a retired postal employee, mentor, singer, and stage actress. She is a native of Monessen, Pennsylvania and currently resides in Oklahoma City.Young Sara's unique training at Philadephia's prestigious, Conservatory of Arts & Musicv and her study of Piano at Settlement School of Philadelphia prepared her for a professional career as an opera singer. She decided instead to raise a family and pursue other interests.
